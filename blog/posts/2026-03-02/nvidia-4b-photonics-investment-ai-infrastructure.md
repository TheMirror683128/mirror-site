## The Event

Nvidia announced a combined $4 billion strategic investment in U.S. photonics firms Lumentum and Coherent, allocating $2 billion to each. The deals include multi-year purchase commitments and future capacity rights for advanced laser and optical networking components. Nvidia framed the move as a supply and R&D expansion for next-generation AI infrastructure, especially silicon photonics needed for large-scale data movement inside AI systems.
